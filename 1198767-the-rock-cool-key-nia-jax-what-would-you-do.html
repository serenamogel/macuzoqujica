The Rock Gets An eXtremely Cool Key

Dwayne Johnson: WWE Legend, Action Movie Star, and the man who’s keeping the hopes and dreams of 2000 blockbuster The 6th Day alive and well. The Rock recently became the new owner of the XFL, and tweeted today that he received the stylish key to the sports league:

The deal is official closed and “the keys” to the XFL have been handed over. I humbly accept and promise to put my callouses to hard work building our new brand. Congrats to my partners @DanyGarciaCo & #GerryCardinale and our sharp, hungry team. #XFLOwner #ForTheLoveOfFootball pic.twitter.com/ctwRlewxxA

— Dwayne Johnson (@TheRock) August 21, 2020

The XFL was restarted by original owner Vince McMahon earlier this year before the global pandemic shut down its plans. Here’s hoping that The Rock can find better luck with the organization.

ABC’s hidden camera program “What Would You Do?” invites WWE Superstar Nia Jax onto the program to talk about her experience with bullying and participate in one of the show’s social experiments. The official description of her segment is reproduced below:

A group of teen girls is verbally bullying a classmate at a park. What will witnesses do? Will they defend the young woman or ignore the situation? Jax joins Quiñones behind-the-scenes and opens up about her own story of overcoming bullying and finding self-confidence.

Nia Jax’s episode airs Tuesday, August 25 at 9 PM ET on ABC.
