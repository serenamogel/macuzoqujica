Benito Raman is a Belgian striker who now plays for Belgian club Anderlecht. He began playing football with Cercle Melle’s youth team at the age of four and stayed for only six months. He has represented Belgium at several age groups on the international stage. He has played for Belgium at the U15, U17, U18, U19, U21, and senior levels. On September 9, 2019, he made his international team debut for Belgium in a Euro 2020 qualifier.
