Counter Energy (Japanese: カウンターエネルギー Counter Energy) is a Special Energy card. It was first released as part of the Crimson Invasion expansion.

Card text

All prints
This card provides Energy.

If you have more Prize cards remaining than your opponent, and if this card is attached to a Pokémon that isn't a Pokémon-GX or Pokémon-EX, this card provides every type of Energy but provides only 2 Energy at a time.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art Secret card in the Crimson Invasion expansion, first released in the Japanese Ultradimensional Beasts expansion, both with an uncredited illustrator. In Japan, the Regular print was included in the GX Ultra Shiny subset and Tag Team GX Deck Build Box.
