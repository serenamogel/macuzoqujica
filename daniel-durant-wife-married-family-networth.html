Get To Know Daniel Durant Family And Early Life

Daniel Durant was born on December 25, 1989, in Detroit, Michigan, US. He was born deaf to his deaf parents as their single child.

Daniel last celebrated his 32nd birthday, and his birth sign is Capricorn.

His biological parents struggled with drug and alcohol addiction. Hence, his paternal aunt Lori Durant and later her wife Mary Engels adopted Daniel when he was still a toddler. He grew up with his two mothers in Duluth, Minnesota.

His aunt, Lori, was the one who taught him how to use sign language.

His grandfather is Donald Stephen Durant (the son of Leonard Joseph Durant and Ida), and his grandmother is Jeannine E. M. Wesley (the daughter of Alvin E. Wesley and Germaine J. Berthiaume).

Daniel attended mainstream schools in Duluth before transferring to the Minnesota State School for the Deaf.

Afterward, he attended Rochester Institute of Technology and Gallaudet University, completing his Computer Science degree in 2014.

He then moved to Los Angeles, California, to join the Deaf West Theatre in 2012 for its production of Cyrano de Bergerac. Shortly after, Daniel started landing acting jobs.

The Durant family is of White ethnicity and French-Canadian descent. They follow Christianity.

What Are Daniel Durant’s Net Worth And Achievements?

Daniel Durant has an estimated net worth of $5.5 million. This number is as reported by Net Worth Leaks.
