Jacky Oh, the longtime partner of “Wild ‘N Out” star D.C. Young Fly, died on June 1, 2023, after undergoing plastic surgery in Miami. She was 32 years old.

Oh had travelled to Miami for a “mommy makeover,” a procedure that typically includes breast augmentation, tummy tuck, and liposuction. She had shared photos on social media of herself posing with Dr Zachary Okhah, the surgeon who would perform the surgery.

On the day of the surgery, Oh experienced complications and went into cardiac arrest. She was rushed to the hospital, but she could not be revived.

The cause of death is still under investigation, but it is believed that Oh may have had an allergic reaction to anaesthesia.

DC Young Fly and Jacky Oh had been together for six years and had three children together. They were planning to get married later this year.

Oh’s death has been met with an outpouring of grief from her friends, family, and fans. DC Young Fly has been particularly vocal about his grief, and he has shared several emotional tributes to his late partner on social media.

In a statement, DC Young Fly said, “Jacky was my best friend, my lover, and the mother of my children. She was the most beautiful, kind, and loving person I’ve ever met. I can’t believe she’s gone. I’m heartbroken.”
