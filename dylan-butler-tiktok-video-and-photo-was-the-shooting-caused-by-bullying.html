Dylan Butler Tiktok Video And Photo: Was The Shooting Caused By Bullying?

 Posted on April 5, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  698 words  |  Martina Birk

The Tiktok video that Dylan Butler posted just before he opened fire at Perry High School has received a lot of attention from the public.

Prior to the rampage that left one person dead and five wounded, including the principal of the school, the 17-year-old is said to have uploaded a brief video and an ominous message on social media.

Around 7:30 in the morning, Dylan Butler is said to have brought a pistol and a shotgun to Perry High School.

After the horrific event, the gunman, a senior at his school, is said to have turned the weapon on himself.

Students hid in offices, took shelter in classrooms, and fled in terror as he opened fire.

In addition to horrifying the on-campus students, the episode attracted a lot of media and public attention.

Butler’s terrifying TikTok video has garnered attention in the meanwhile. Let’s take a closer look at the video and other pictures on his social media.

Dylan Butler Images And Videos On Tiktok

Dylan Butler shared a terrifying video on the well-known TikTok social media network.

He posted the footage from the school’s restroom just before he started a terrifying shooting rampage on Thursday at exactly 7:47 in the morning.

The brief clip’s menacing title, “Now we wait,” added to the eerie mood.

The background music, Stray Bullet, added to the spooky atmosphere.

For those who don’t know, this song is by the well-known German rock group KMFDM.

Some of the song’s most unsettling lyrics include “Stray bullet, from the barrel of love” and “I’m your nightmare coming true, I am your worst enemy.”

The video’s setting and these words appeared to allude to the approaching catastrophe.

Authorities found many other images of Butler throughout their inquiry.

His several gun poses in these pictures added credence to the idea that he was planning a violent crime.

Butler is suspected by the authorities of having meant to do much more damage. An improvised explosive device that was recovered on his person served as evidence for this.

This revelation heightens the tragedy of the occurrence by implying that Butler planned to murder a great number of people.

The community is still in shock over the whole thing, finding it difficult to accept this horrific act of violence.

Dylan Butler Had Enough Of Being Mistreated

Dylan Butler has long struggled with the agony of bullying, as has been made public.

The horrific shooting incident’s motivation has not yet been revealed by the authorities.

Speaking with The Associated Press, two of Butler’s acquaintances described a young guy who had endured years of unrelenting bullying.

Butler’s 17-year-old companion Yesenia Roeder revealed her pain by stating, “He was hurting.” He became weary. He had had enough of the intimidation and harassment.

Yesenia’s sister Khamya Hall echoed the same feelings. She said that Butler and his younger sister were the targets of ongoing harassment and bullying.

The sisters claim that Butler’s stance was finally reached after their parents brought up this matter at the school.

And a family that knew Butler also gave a similar account of him.

They recalled him as a reserved man who had experienced bullying since he was in elementary school.

It should be mentioned, however, that the official reason for the outburst has not yet been disclosed.
