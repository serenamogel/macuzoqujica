Frontier Airlines offering 'GoWild!' all-you-can-fly fall, winter pass

 Posted on March 19, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  132 words  |  Aldo Pusey

LAS VEGAS (KSNV) — Summer is here, but one airline is already kicking off travel plans for next season.

Frontier Airlines is offering its GoWild! All-You-Can-Fly Fall & Winter Pass for $299 until June 13.

The promotion includes unlimited travel from Sept. 2, 2023, through Feb. 29, 2024, with access to more than 85 U.S. and international U.S. destinations.

“Whether you want to soak up some sun on the beach or hit the ski slopes, the GoWild! All-You-Can-Fly Fall & Winter pass is an unprecedented bargain at just $299 while supplies last,” said Daniel Shurz, senior vice president, commercial, Frontier Airlines in a news release. “Combined with the special early booking promotion, this is a unique opportunity to enjoy some supreme savings on fall and winter travel.”
