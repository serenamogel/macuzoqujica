Married at First Sight: Season 10

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  815 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Episode 1 Aired Jan 1, 2020 First Comes Marriage, Then Comes Love Five couples have only two weeks to plan their weddings, celebrate their final days of being single, and reveal to their family and friends that they are getting married to a stranger. Details Episode 2 Aired Jan 8, 2020 Here Comes the Stranger Five couples get ready to walk down the aisle; while the first three couples celebrate their marriages with the help of family and friends, two couples still remain to take the giant leap of faith. Details Episode 3 Aired Jan 15, 2020 I Married a Stranger All five couples dance the night away at their wedding receptions, before spending their first night together as husband and wife. Details Episode 4 Aired Jan 22, 2020 One Night Spouse After waking up next to someone they met less than 24 hours ago, each spouse will meet individually with their new stranger in-laws, before being whisked away on their honeymoon in Panama. Details Episode 5 Aired Jan 29, 2020 Trouble in Paradise Some of the newlyweds live out their fairy-tale love story on the tropical beaches of Panama, while other couples get off to a rocky start; tensions rise as three couples struggle to find common ground with their stranger spouses. Details Episode 6 Aired Feb 5, 2020 In Seasickness and in Health Four pairs of newlyweds make the most of their honeymoon with a romantic catamaran trip, while one couple remains on shore to heal their fractured marriage; despite sunny skies, there are rough seas ahead for two pairs of stranger spouses. Details Episode 7 Aired Feb 12, 2020 New Wife, New Life With the thrill of the honeymoon over, five pairs of newlyweds return to Washington, D.C., to begin living together; the pressure of moving in proves to be too much for two couples, while others enjoy discovering new surprises about their new spouse. Details Episode 8 Aired Feb 19, 2020 Home Is Where Your Spouse Is Four pairs of newlyweds settle into married life in their new homes, while one pair struggles to find its footing; some friends are thrilled to hear how well married life is going, while others refuse to keep quiet about their concerns. Details Episode 9 Aired Feb 26, 2020 The L Word After marrying a stranger, five couples grow toward love with their stranger spouse by planning romantic surprises; for one couple, an unexpected surprise might be the final straw for their marriage. Details Episode 10 Aired Mar 4, 2020 I Want You to Want Me As five pairs of newlyweds adjust to married life in their own ways, the group attends a cooking class together; while passion heats up between some couples, one couple completely melts down. Details Episode 11 Aired Mar 11, 2020 Anniversaries and Secrets One-month wedding anniversaries are celebrated as the experiment reaches the crucial halfway point; while some couples walk hand-in-hand toward a future together, others worry that this first milestone may be the only anniversary they ever celebrate. Details Episode 12 Aired Mar 18, 2020 Move In or Move On All five couples sit down to take a hard look at their marriages and decide what it will take for them to stay committed until decision day; some couples can't wait to recommit, but for others the deal breakers may prove too much to handle. Details Episode 13 Aired Mar 25, 2020 Secrets and Lies The five married couples must embrace vulnerability, revisit their past, and expose their deepest truths; some discover new sparks, while others find themselves ready to walk away completely. Details Episode 14 Aired Apr 1, 2020 Fight or Flight? After one couple reaches the end of the road in their marriage, the remaining four couples go on a retreat where close quarters force them to confront their issues; tempers flare as the clock winds down to Decision Day. Details Episode 15 Aired Apr 8, 2020 Forever or Never As the eight-week experiment draws to a close and Decision Day looms, the four remaining couples have just a few days left to do some final soul-searching, consult with friends and family, and decide if they want to stay married or get a divorce. Details Episode 16 Aired Apr 15, 2020 Until Decision Day Do We Part The eight-week experiment comes to an end, as the five couples sit down with the experts to decide whether to stay married or get a divorce; some couples have their eyes on the future, while other couples are determined to get answers about the past. Details Episode 17 Aired Apr 22, 2020 Season 10 Reunion Five months after decision day, the five Washington, D.C., couples who got married at first sight reunite to look back on the good times and the hard times, discuss the ups and downs of their marriage, and reveal if they are still married.
