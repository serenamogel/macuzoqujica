Learn All About Aaron Sorkin And Paulina Porizkova's Dating Life, Are They Married?

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  424 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

American playwright, Aaron Sorkin and Paulina Porizkova make one hell of a couple. They are both incredibly successful and popular in their respective careers. Sorkin and Porizkova made their relationship official on the red carpet of the 2021 Oscars and sent their fans in an uproar. 

Sorkin was nominated for “The Trial of the Chicago 7” in the 2021 Oscars. While Paulina is also an accomplished model with a plethora of credits to her name. Aaron is Paulina’s first public relationship after she lost her husband to disease in 2019. 

Aaron Sorkin and Paulina Porizkova’s Dating Life

Fans suspected that Sorkin and Porizkova were dating for a few years, however, the secretive couple rarely let fans into their personal lives. 

This all changed at the 2021 Oscars when Paulina accompanied Aaron to the red carpet as he hoped to win an Oscar for his screenplay. Their relationship was leaked by Page Six prior to their planned debut. It was revealed that they had been dating for a few months. Paulina has been focusing all her energy on dating her newfound love. 

As a matter of fact, the Central European model also regularly posts updates about going on dates with Aaron. As of April 2021, the pair have not revealed any plans to get married soon. However, they seem to be quite fond of each other and might take the next step in their relationship in the near future. 

Both Aaron And Paulina Were Married In The Past 

Paulina got hitched to Ric Ocasek on 23 August 1989, he was the lead singer for the band The Cars. Together they had two sons Jonathan Raven Ocasek (born 4 November 1993) and Oliver Ocasek (born 1999). 

The pair were together for almost 30 years before they separated in 2018. Ric passed away in 2019 after complications from an unspecified surgery. He died while they were in the process of a divorce, due to this he had disinherited her from his will. 

Similarly, Aaron was also married in the past to Julia Bingham. They first got married in 1996, while their marriage was a celebrated affair, his workaholic nature and addiction to drugs as part of the cause of their divorce. He has a daughter named Roxy Sorkin, born on 17 November 2000. 

Ex-Girlfriend Of Aaron Sorkin

Later, Sorkin was in a serious relationship with Junie Chenoweth's daughter, Kristin Chenoweth, a well-known American actress. Their much-publicized relationship ended after a few years. Other than this, he was also rumored to be dating Maureen Dowd and actress Kristin Davis.
