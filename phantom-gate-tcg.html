Pokémon TCG: XY—Phantom Forces (Japanese: ファントムゲート Phantom Gate) is the name given to the fourth main expansion of cards from the XY Series of the Pokémon Trading Card Game (the XY Era in Japan). The set continues to feature Generation VI Pokémon, Pokémon-EX, and M Pokémon-EX in the card game.

Information

Phantom Gate logo

Phantom Forces is the name given to the fourth main expansion of the XY Series of the Pokémon Trading Card Game. In Japan, it was released as Phantom Gate, the fourth expansion in the Pokémon Card Game XY Era. It is based on Pokémon X and Y, featuring Generation VI Pokémon and the Shadow Pokémon Gengar. The English expansion was released on November 5, 2014, while the Japanese expansion was released on September 13, 2014.

Phantom Forces introduced Spirit Link, Pokémon Tool cards that allowed Pokémon to Mega Evolve without ending the player's turn.

The set has a light Team Flare theme, featuring major characters as Supporter cards, a group of Item cards designated as Team Flare Gear and two Pokémon Tool cards that are Team Flare Hyper Gear. The Team Flare Hyper Gear cards are unique as Tools that are attached to the opponent's Pokémon in order to hinder them.

Languages this set is released in

The Phantom Gate set is released in Japanese and Korean. The Japanese set is available in both 1st and unlimited edition, and the Korean set is only available in unlimited edition. The Phantom Forces set is released in English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, and Russian. Cards in the English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, and Russian sets were also available as Reverse Holos. The Portuguese version of this set lacked any Reverse Holos.
