About Webster Canvas

  • Canvas Webster is a web-based learning management system (LMS) used by Webster for online teaching and learning. Webster Canvas enables the Webster faculty to use web-based collaborative and assessment technologies to enhance the teaching and learning experience.
  • Webster Canvas includes a variety of customizable course creation and management tools, course and user analytics and statistics, and internal communication tools.
  • Webster Canvas is a web-based software that allows institutions to manage digital learning,
  • Although, Canvas is primarily a web-based software, any user can access Canvas on a mobile device from the Canvas Teacher, Canvas Student, and Canvas Parent apps. Learn more about the Canvas mobile apps.

Uses Of Webster Canvas

  • With Webster Canvas, educators can create and present online learning materials and assess student learning online, as well as allow students to engage in courses and receive feedback about skill development and learning achievement.
  • Webster Canvas allows teaching staff to build and add tools to their course site to more specifically meet their particular pedagogical needs, and administration can build and add tools specific to Webster systems.
  • Instructors can use this service to design full-featured online course environments for participants of the classes. Students can also access course materials, collaborate with others, engage with interactive assessments and assignments, and track their progress in a course gradebook.
  • It is used by learning institutions, educators, and students of Webster to access and manage online course learning materials and communicate about skill development and learning achievement.

Webster Canvas Login Requirements

  • You will be able to access Webster Canvas from any computer, tablet, or mobile device with internet connectivity.

How To Access Webster Canvas

Webster Canvas Login

  • First visit the Webster canvas login portal at: https://worldclassroom.webster.edu/
  • Then enter your username and password
  • Then click on the login button
  • Your Webster Canvas dashboard will be opened
  • Use the menus on the page to access a feature, for instance you can Click on ‘Courses’ or ‘My Courses’ to view all courses assigned to your class

    • Webster Canvas App

    Webster Canvas Student App

    • To access the Webster Canvas Student App, first visit Google PlayStore or Apple Store to download and install the Canvas App.
    • Afterwards open the app and tap on “Find my school”
    • Then type the name of your school (ie Webster)
    • A list of schools will be displayed, then tap Webster
    • The Webster canvas login page will be displayed
    • Then enter your username and password to sign into your Webster canvas
