Wolfgang Pluck | Career

Puck’s career started when he relocated to France and worked in some of the best restaurants in the country.

Maxim’s in Paris, the Hotel de Paris in Monaco, and the Michelin 3-starred L’Oustau de Baumanière in Provence were his workplaces in France.

He decided to migrate to the USA to pursue new opportunities.

His first job was in the Indianapolis restaurant La Tour, where he worked from 1973 to 1975 before moving to Los Angeles.

After working in Ma Maison, Pluck established himself as a refined gourmet cook.

His extensive knowledge of Californian cuisine and outstanding culinary abilities made the restaurant extremely popular.

In 1982, Wolfgang started his restaurant, Spago, on the Sunset Strip in West Hollywood. From the beginning, the restaurant was a huge success.

Since then, Puck opened more restaurants during the next few years, including Chinois on Main in Santa Monica in 1983 and Postrio in San Francisco’s Union Square in 1989.

FAQs

What is Wolfgang Puck’s signature dish?

Wolfgang Puck’s House Smoked Salmon Pizza is his signature dish. In addition, Classic Chicken Pot Pie and Catalan Fire Roasted Lamb Rack are two of his most famous dishes.

What is a popular quote from Wolfgang Puck?

Several of his quotes are famous. But one particular quote that states, “For me, cooking is an expression of the land where you are and the culture of that place,” is probably his best quote.

Did Wolfgang Puck join the university?

Around 2018, he enrolled in Harvard Business School’s Owner/President Management program; an accomplishment Puck says he’s proud of given that he started cooking at the age of 14 and never finished high school or college.
